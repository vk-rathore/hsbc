What is an SIP?

A regular investment plan known as Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) aloows you to invest a fixed amount in mutual funds at regular intervals.

Benifits of an SIP?

  • Rupee-cost averaging
  • No need for market timing
  • Compounding benifits
  • Aim to beat inflation
  • Instill investment discipline
    •

    Know More

    Starting a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) can be a wise decision for many reasons. Here are some of the key benefits to consider:

    • Rupee-cost averaging
    • No need for market timing
    • Compounding benefits
    • Aim to beat inflation
    • Instill investment discipline

    Overall, starting an SIP can be a great way to begin investing and grow wealth over the long-term. It offers numerous benefits, including regular investment, the power of compounding, diversification, and flexibility.
